Legend:
  • Equipment on site: mark the box indicating the equipment in color BLUE
  • Red Tag: mark the box indicating the equipment in color PURPLE
  • Cable Pulling: mark the line or device in color GRAY
  • Connected: mark the line or device in color ORANGE
  • Yellow Tag: mark the box indicating the equipment in color YELLOW
  • Conditional Yellow Tag: mark the box indicating the equipment in color BROWN
  • Energized: marks the line or device in RED for energized, or GREEN for open circuit breaker or switch
